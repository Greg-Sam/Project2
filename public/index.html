CodeBook

A simple app to help you keep track of all your coding accomplishments. CodeBook allows you to compete and compare your progress with your classmates and coworkers. Help celebrate small or large victories to encourage your friends on their coding journey!

About Us/Our App

We are a team of five developers currently attending the UCI Full Stack coding bootcamp. We have learned so much in the last seven weeks and are eager to showcase our skills with this project.

This app allows you to showcase your accomplishments with other developers as well as get help with your programming questions. This project demonstrates our abilities in creating a full stack web application after programming for just 6 weeks. We are very excited for you to give our application a try!
